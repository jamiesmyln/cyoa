Chapter 1

Thunder rumbles distantly as the soft plinking of raindrops against the window stirs you into consciousness. The covers of your bed are fantastically soft and it’s very tempting to give in to the melancholy weather’s call for sleep. Suddenly, a brisk knock on your bedroom door pulls you from bed. You stumble into your slippers, put on your robe, and open the bedroom door.

A cheerful looking elderly man in scholar’s robes is bustling with excitement. You weren’t quite prepared for this amount of energy at this time of morning, but his attitude is contagious.

Archimedes: Oh, good! You’re awake, your highness! This letter arrived for you this morning and the messenger seemed quite distressed. She was wearing the purple and silver colors of the neighboring kingdom, Vendeshire. It must be urgent!
